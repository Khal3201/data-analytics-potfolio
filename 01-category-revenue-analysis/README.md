## Executive Summary

An analysis was conducted on the revenue generated by the movie categories available in the "Sakila" database. The data indicates that the "Sports" category accounts for the highest revenue, followed by "Documentaries," "Animation," and "Games"; together, these categories represent 31.8% of total revenue and rentals, signaling high customer demand.

In contrast, categories such as "Classics," "Horror," and "Children's" account for only 18.4% of revenue. Given this lower share, it would be advisable to evaluate whether to expand the catalog in these areas or focus instead on the best-performing categories.


---

## Business Recommendation

My recommendation is to prioritize the acquisition and promotion of movies in the most profitable categories while analyzing the reasons behind the underperformance of the less profitable ones.
